About Us

- PRESENTIA -

DESCRIPTION:

PRESENTIA is an event where the participants are required to make a paper about a certain topic or area, and then present it in front of a jury, following which it will be rated and judged based on their performance. The purpose of a paper presentation is to enhance a particular student’s ability in the art of academic writing or making papers in English and to present it. It also helps broaden the minds of the presenters.

RULES
  • Maximum number of participants in a Team is 3 and everyone must be present on the event day.
  • Paper should be in IEEE Format. Paper must contain index, list of figures, list of tables, abstract, introduction, point wise description of subject and conclusion, Result and references.
  • The topics that the papers can be chosen must focus on one particular topic from technical domain.
  • Presentation document must be between 8-15 pages.
  • Kindly bring your PowerPoint presentation on a pen-drive.
  • The mail with submissions should contain: Title of the paper, abstract, references, Name and Mobile number of the participants, Name of the College
  • Soft-copies of the submitted paper and PowerPoint presentations must be in the .docx Format and should be submitted on or before 15.03.2024
  • We will contact you on 17.03.24 if you're is shortlisted for presentation on the event day.
  • Kindly Mail your abstract, paper and ppt to paperpresentationaicon@gmail.com
  • The teams will get 15 minutes for presentation and followed by question and answer session.
  • The decision of judges will be final and no arguments or appeal will be entertained.
  • Bring your college ID Card on event day, no entries will be permitted without the ID Card.

NOTE:Winners of this event will be rewarded with cash prize and certificates.
